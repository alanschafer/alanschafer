Mission

VON is a research initiative dedicated to the clinical investigation of belief systems, cognitive frameworks, and trauma-induced psychological phenomena. Our focus is on the structural analysis of memory, meaning-making, and the neurological underpinnings of conviction. We employ rigorous methodologies to deconstruct and document the mechanics of human belief.
